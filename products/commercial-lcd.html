Key Features

Why Choose Commercial LCD

24/7 Reliability

Commercial-grade panels designed for continuous operation without image burn-in or degradation.

High Brightness

Up to 700 nits brightness ensures visibility in bright environments and storefronts.

Video Wall Ready

Ultra-narrow bezels and daisy-chain capability for seamless multi-display video walls.

Built-in SoC

Integrated System-on-Chip eliminates need for external media players in many applications.

Remote Management

LAN/RS232 control for remote power, input switching, and diagnostics across networks.

Portrait & Landscape

Install in any orientation with automatic content adjustment and thermal management.

Advanced Cooling

Internal fans and heat dissipation systems for reliable operation in enclosed spaces.

Multiple Inputs

HDMI, DisplayPort, VGA, USB, and OPS slot for versatile connectivity options.

Built for Business

Unlike consumer TVs, our commercial displays are engineered for demanding business environments. Commercial-grade panels, robust power supplies, and industrial components ensure years of reliable 24/7 operation.

  • 50,000+ hour panel lifespan
  • Anti-burn-in technology
  • Industrial-grade components

Video Wall Excellence

Create stunning video walls of any size with our ultra-narrow bezel displays. With bezels as thin as 0.88mm, your content appears seamless across multiple screens for maximum visual impact.

  • 0.88mm ultra-narrow bezel
  • Daisy-chain up to 25 displays
  • Hardware-based video wall processor

Smart Signage Platform

The built-in System-on-Chip (SoC) runs Android or webOS, enabling standalone digital signage without external players. Schedule content, manage playlists, and update remotely through cloud-based CMS.

  • Built-in media player
  • Cloud CMS compatible
  • Scheduled content playback

Enterprise Control

Centrally manage hundreds of displays from a single dashboard. Remote monitoring provides real-time status, power consumption, and alerts for proactive maintenance across your entire network.

  • Centralized management software
  • Real-time health monitoring
  • Automated scheduling
