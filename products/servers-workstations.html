Key Features

Why Choose Our Server Solutions

Multi-Core Processing

Intel Xeon and AMD EPYC processors with up to 128 cores for handling the most demanding enterprise workloads.

Massive Memory

Support for up to 4TB of ECC DDR5 RAM for virtualization, databases, and in-memory computing applications.

Built-in Redundancy

Hot-swappable drives, redundant power supplies, and RAID configurations ensure zero downtime for critical operations.

Scalable Architecture

Easily scale compute, storage, and memory resources as your business grows without replacing infrastructure.

Hardware Security

TPM 2.0, secure boot, silicon root of trust, and encrypted drives protect against physical and cyber threats.

Remote Management

iLO, iDRAC, and IPMI remote management tools for monitoring, diagnostics, and out-of-band administration.

NVMe Storage

Ultra-fast NVMe SSD arrays with RAID support delivering millions of IOPS for data-intensive applications.

Energy Efficient

80 PLUS Titanium certified power supplies and intelligent cooling systems reduce operating costs and carbon footprint.

Product Categories

Server & Workstation Solutions

Rack Servers

High-density rack-mount servers designed for data centers and server rooms. Optimized for compute-intensive workloads with maximum performance per rack unit.

  • 1U, 2U, and 4U form factors
  • Dual-socket configurations up to 128 cores
  • Hot-swappable drives & redundant PSUs

Tower Servers

Standalone tower servers ideal for small and medium businesses that don't have dedicated server rooms. Quiet operation with enterprise-grade reliability in a familiar desktop form factor.

  • No rack infrastructure required
  • Quiet acoustic design for office environments
  • Easy to deploy and maintain

Blade Servers

Ultra-dense modular blade systems for large-scale data center deployments. Shared power, cooling, and networking reduce cost per node while maximizing compute density.

  • Maximum compute density per rack
  • Shared infrastructure reduces TCO
  • Centralized management for all blades

Storage Servers & NAS

Dedicated storage solutions for centralized data management, backup, and archival. From entry-level NAS to enterprise SAN systems with petabyte-scale capacity.

  • SAN, NAS, and unified storage options
  • RAID 5/6/10 for data protection
  • Scalable from terabytes to petabytes

Professional Workstations

High-performance workstations for CAD/CAM, 3D rendering, video editing, AI/ML development, and scientific computing. ISV-certified for professional applications.

  • NVIDIA RTX / AMD Radeon Pro GPUs
  • ISV certified for AutoCAD, SolidWorks, Adobe
  • Up to 128GB ECC RAM

Hyper-Converged Infrastructure (HCI)

Software-defined infrastructure combining compute, storage, and networking into a single appliance. Simplify your data center with easy deployment and unified management.

  • Compute + Storage + Networking in one
  • Simple scale-out by adding nodes
  • Built-in virtualization & data protection

University & Campus Data Centers

Purpose-built server infrastructure for educational institutions. Host LMS platforms, student portals, examination systems, and research databases on secure, on-premise servers tailored for academic environments.

  • LMS & ERP hosting (Moodle, SAP, Oracle)
  • Student information system (SIS) servers
  • Examination & result processing infrastructure
  • Scalable for growing student populations

Research & HPC Clusters

High-performance computing clusters for academic research. GPU-accelerated servers for AI/ML research, scientific simulations, bioinformatics, and engineering analysis used by universities and research institutions.

  • Multi-GPU nodes for AI & deep learning
  • Parallel computing for scientific simulations
  • Shared cluster access for faculty & students
  • Integration with research cloud platforms
