Product Categories

Microphone Solutions

Ceiling Microphone Arrays

Beamforming ceiling microphones provide hands-free audio capture for meeting rooms and classrooms. Advanced DSP automatically steers audio beams to active talkers while rejecting noise and echo.

  • 360-degree room coverage
  • Automatic voice tracking
  • Built-in AEC and noise reduction

Table Microphones

Desktop and boundary microphones for conference tables and lecterns. Low-profile designs with wide pickup patterns ensure everyone at the table is heard clearly.

  • Gooseneck and boundary options
  • Touch-to-mute functionality
  • Dante and USB connectivity

Wireless Handheld & Lavalier

Digital wireless microphone systems for presentations, events, and training. Reliable RF performance with automatic frequency coordination for multi-channel deployments.

  • Up to 64 channels simultaneous
  • Rechargeable lithium-ion batteries
  • Bodypack and handheld options

Discussion & Voting Systems

Digital conference systems for boardrooms, council chambers, and government applications. Integrated voting, interpretation, and speaker management with central control.

  • Electronic voting capability
  • Interpretation channels
  • Automatic camera tracking
