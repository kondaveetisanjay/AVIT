Deep Black Non-Glare Technology

Sony's exclusive Deep Black Non-Glare Coating dramatically reduces ambient light reflections while maintaining deep, rich black levels. This technology ensures optimal viewing in challenging lighting conditions such as brightly-lit lobbies and retail spaces.

  • Reduces reflections by up to 90%
  • Maintains contrast in bright environments
  • Anti-fingerprint surface treatment

TRILUMINOS PRO Color

TRILUMINOS PRO technology uses Sony's advanced color reproduction techniques to display a wider range of colors than conventional displays. Experience natural reds, vivid greens, and deep blues that bring content to life.

  • Wide color gamut coverage
  • Natural skin tone reproduction
  • HDR10 and HLG support

4K HDR X1 Processor

The 4K HDR Processor X1 delivers Sony's renowned picture quality with intelligent signal processing. Advanced algorithms enhance every frame with optimal contrast, color, and detail enhancement.

  • Real-time object-based processing
  • 4K upscaling for SD/HD content
  • Dynamic contrast enhancement

Professional Integration

Designed for enterprise and AV integrator workflows with comprehensive control options. IP Control, RS-232C, and IR compatibility ensure seamless integration with existing infrastructure.

  • IP-based control and monitoring
  • RS-232C serial control
  • Pro Mode settings memory
