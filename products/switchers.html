Key Features

Why Choose Our AV Switchers

Seamless Switching

Glitch-free transitions between multiple AV sources with zero signal dropout for uninterrupted presentations.

4K HDR Pass-through

Full support for 4K@60Hz 4:4:4 and HDR content pass-through ensuring pristine image quality on every display.

Auto-Detect Sources

Intelligent source detection automatically switches to newly connected devices for effortless plug-and-play operation.

Matrix Routing

Route any input to any output independently, enabling flexible multi-display configurations from a single system.

PoE Support

Power over Ethernet capability simplifies installation by delivering power and signal over a single cable run.

RS-232/IP Control

Full integration with third-party control systems via RS-232, IP, and IR for centralized AV management.

Audio De-embedding

Extract and route audio independently from video signals for flexible sound system integration.

Rack Mountable

Standard 19" rack-mount form factors for clean, organized installations in equipment closets and AV racks.

Product Categories

Switcher Solutions

HDMI Switchers

Multi-input HDMI switchers for selecting between multiple source devices on a single display. Ideal for conference rooms, huddle spaces, and desktop setups requiring quick source selection.

  • 4K@60Hz HDMI 2.0 support
  • Auto-switching on signal detection
  • HDCP 2.2 compliant

Matrix Switchers

Any-input-to-any-output matrix routing for complex AV environments. Route multiple sources to multiple displays independently with full signal integrity and zero latency.

  • Flexible input/output configurations (4x4 to 32x32)
  • HDMI, HDBaseT, and fiber options
  • Independent audio routing

Presentation Switchers

All-in-one presentation systems combining switching, scaling, and audio mixing in a single unit. Built-in scaling ensures seamless display from any laptop or device resolution.

  • Built-in scaler and audio DSP
  • HDMI, VGA, USB-C inputs
  • One-touch presentation mode

Classroom AV Switchers

Purpose-built switching systems for education designed for simplicity and reliability in teaching environments. Enable teachers to focus on instruction, not technology.

  • One-touch source switching for teachers
  • Auto-switch between laptop, document camera & interactive panel
  • Centralized AV control for smart classrooms

Video Wall Controllers

Advanced video wall processing systems for driving multi-display configurations. Manage content across video walls with flexible windowing, scaling, and layout control.

  • Multi-window layout management
  • Real-time source switching & PiP
  • Supports up to 4K per output
