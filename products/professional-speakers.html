Product Categories

Speaker Solutions

Ceiling Speakers

Discreet in-ceiling speaker systems ideal for offices, classrooms, and retail spaces. Available in various sizes from 4" to 8" drivers with options for low-profile designs that blend into any ceiling.

  • 4", 6", and 8" driver options
  • Tile bridge and back-can accessories
  • 70V/100V and low-impedance models

Surface Mount Speakers

Wall and surface-mounted speakers for flexible placement in conference rooms, lobbies, and auditoriums. Compact designs with powerful output for speech reinforcement and background music.

  • Multiple mounting options
  • Weather-resistant models available
  • Matching subwoofers for full-range sound

Column & Line Array Speakers

Steerable column speakers provide precise audio coverage for challenging acoustic environments. Digital beam steering technology directs sound exactly where needed while minimizing reflections.

  • Digital beam steering
  • Ideal for reverberant spaces
  • Speech intelligibility optimization

Portable PA Systems

All-in-one portable sound systems for training rooms, presentations, and events. Battery-powered options with wireless microphone compatibility for maximum flexibility.

  • Built-in mixer and amplifier
  • Bluetooth streaming
  • Wireless mic compatibility
