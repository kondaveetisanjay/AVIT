Room Solutions

By Room Size

Focus Rooms (1-4 People)

Compact all-in-one solutions for personal offices and small huddle spaces. USB-connected devices that work seamlessly with any laptop while maintaining enterprise-grade quality.

  • All-in-one video bars
  • USB plug-and-play setup
  • Built-in speakers and mics

Small Rooms (4-8 People)

Integrated room systems with dedicated compute, 4K camera, and professional audio. Ideal for small meeting rooms and collaboration spaces with Teams or Zoom Rooms.

  • Dedicated room compute
  • Wide-angle 4K camera
  • Touch controller included

Medium Rooms (8-14 People)

Modular room systems with PTZ cameras, table microphones, and powerful speakers. Scalable configurations for conference rooms and training spaces.

  • PTZ cameras with auto-tracking
  • Expandable microphone arrays
  • Dual display support

Large Rooms (14+ People)

Enterprise room solutions for boardrooms and large meeting spaces. Multiple cameras, ceiling microphone arrays, and premium audio for executive-level meetings.

  • Multi-camera configurations
  • Ceiling microphone arrays
  • Premium DSP audio processing
