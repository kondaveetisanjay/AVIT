Key Features

Why Choose Our WiFi Solutions

WiFi 6E/7 Ready

Next-generation wireless standards delivering multi-gigabit speeds, lower latency, and support for more simultaneous devices.

Cloud Management

Centralized cloud-based dashboards for monitoring, configuration, and troubleshooting across all access points from anywhere.

Seamless Roaming

802.11r/k/v fast roaming protocols ensure uninterrupted connectivity as users move between access points.

WPA3 Security

Latest WPA3 encryption with advanced threat detection, rogue AP isolation, and WIDS/WIPS protection.

MU-MIMO

Multi-User MIMO technology enables simultaneous data streams to multiple devices for maximum throughput.

PoE Powered

Power over Ethernet eliminates the need for separate power outlets, simplifying installation and reducing cabling costs.

Guest Networks

Isolated guest SSIDs with captive portal, bandwidth limits, and customizable splash pages for visitor access.

Load Balancing

Intelligent client steering and band steering distribute traffic evenly across APs and frequency bands.

Product Categories

WiFi Solutions

Enterprise Access Points

High-performance ceiling and wall-mount access points designed for dense office environments. Enterprise-grade reliability with centralized management and advanced security features.

  • WiFi 6E with tri-band radios
  • Up to 200+ concurrent clients per AP
  • Cloud or on-premise controller options

Mesh WiFi Systems

Self-healing mesh networks that provide seamless blanket coverage across large spaces. Easy to deploy with automatic optimization and zero-touch provisioning.

  • Self-forming mesh topology
  • Dedicated wireless backhaul
  • Automatic failover and recovery

Outdoor Access Points

Weather-resistant outdoor APs built for harsh environments. IP67-rated enclosures with extended-range antennas for campuses, stadiums, and public spaces.

  • IP67 weatherproof rating
  • Extended range with directional antennas
  • Point-to-point and point-to-multipoint modes

Campus & Education WiFi

High-density classroom WiFi with campus-wide coverage designed for educational institutions. Student device management and content filtering for schools ensure safe, reliable connectivity.

  • High-density classroom WiFi support
  • Student device management and BYOD policies
  • Content filtering for schools and compliance

SMB Wireless Solutions

Cost-effective wireless solutions for small and medium businesses. Simple setup, intuitive management, and enterprise-level features without the complexity.

  • Easy plug-and-play deployment
  • App-based management
  • Affordable scalability
