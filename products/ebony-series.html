Key Features

Why Choose Ebony Series

Moisture-Proof

Advanced protection against humidity and moisture for reliable performance in any environment.

Fanless Design

Silent operation with superior heat dissipation technology, no moving parts means no noise.

Better Heat Dissipation

Innovative thermal management ensures optimal performance even during extended use.

Dust-Proof

IP65 rated protection safeguards your display, preserving image quality and extending lifespan.

Excellent Flatness

Precision engineering ensures perfectly flat surface for seamless visual presentation.

Long Lifetime

100,000 hours LED lifetime ensures years of reliable, high-quality display performance.

Low Failure Rate

Premium COB technology with 10,000 hours MTBF for maximum uptime and reliability.

User Safety

Designed with safety in mind, meeting international standards for commercial installations.

Infinite Depth, Stunning Clarity

Ebony's exceptional 100000:1 contrast ratio delivers images with extraordinary depth and detail, bringing your visuals to life. Experience the difference between conventional 10000:1 contrast and Ebony's superior performance.

  • 10x better contrast than conventional displays
  • Deeper blacks for enhanced visual depth
  • True-to-life color reproduction

Dominate Any Space

Ebony's 1000 nits of peak brightness defy ambient light, ensuring vibrant colors and sharp images in any environment. From bright lobbies to sunlit conference rooms, your content always shines.

  • 1000 nits peak brightness
  • Vibrant colors in any lighting
  • Perfect for high ambient light areas

Pure Blacks, Perfect Picture

Experience the ultimate in visual immersion with Ebony's full black coating technology. Combined with Chip-on-Board (COB) technology, it eliminates light bleed for deeper blacks and richer colors.

  • Full black coating technology
  • COB (Chip on Board) LED technology
  • Zero light bleed for perfect blacks

Built to Endure

The robust IP65/IP41 rating shields your display from the elements, ensuring reliable performance in any setting. Micro LED technology provides superior durability compared to traditional SMD displays.

  • IP65 protection (0.9mm - 1.5mm models)
  • IP41 protection (1.8mm - 2.5mm models)
  • Micro LED vs SMD superiority

Sleek and Seamless

The cable-less block assembly design simplifies installation, creating a sleek and modern look. Front-serviceable modules make maintenance quick and easy without disrupting your installation.

  • Cable-less design for clean aesthetics
  • Block assembly for easy installation
  • Front-serviceable modules
