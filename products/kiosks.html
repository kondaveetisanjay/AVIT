Support Get in Touch

Smart Information. Seamless Interaction.

Our kiosks are thoughtfully engineered to deliver exceptional performance, durability, and user engagement in demanding commercial environments. Designed for continuous 24/7 operation, they feature a robust steel structure with a rust-resistant powder-coated finish and enhanced internal protection for long-term reliability.

The integration of advanced IR touch technology ensures precise, responsive multi-touch interaction, creating an intuitive and seamless user experience. An added layer of tempered glass enhances durability while maintaining smooth and consistent screen performance over extended use.

To further elevate visual clarity, the display is optimized to minimize ripple distortion, ensuring a clean and stable viewing experience. Additionally, an optional cloud-based content management system (CMS) provides centralized control and flexible scheduling, enabling effortless management of digital content with maximum efficiency and scalability.

The kiosks are available in a wide range of display sizes, including 32", 43", 55", 65", 75", 86", and 98", allowing flexible deployment across various commercial environments and space requirements.

A - Frame Kiosk

Ultra-Slim. Effortlessly Portable.

This ultra-slim, lightweight display solution is designed for maximum portability and versatility, featuring a streamlined foldable stand that allows for effortless setup, transport, and storage. Built with advanced IPS panel technology, it delivers vibrant, true-to-life colors with wide 178° viewing angles, enhanced brightness, and improved contrast through high-performance LED backlighting for long-lasting efficiency.

Its sleek A-frame construction, durable metal structure, and tempered glass front ensure both visual appeal and structural strength, making it ideal for space-conscious environments. Integrated surround sound provides an engaging audiovisual experience with support for multiple media formats, while plug-and-play functionality enables quick and simple content uploads via USB for seamless operation.

Engineered for reliable 24/7 public use, the commercial-grade LCD panel offers over 30,000 hours of performance with a secure design that minimizes tampering. Additionally, a cloud-based CMS allows remote content management and updates, supported by a versatile operating system with compatibility for Windows and third-party applications to meet diverse business requirements.
