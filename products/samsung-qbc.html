Key Features

Why Choose Samsung QBC Series

Dynamic Crystal Color

Experience vivid, true-to-life colors with Samsung's Dynamic Crystal Color technology for stunning visual impact.

Ultra-Slim 28.5mm Design

Sleek, space-saving profile that blends seamlessly into any environment without compromising on performance.

4000:1 Contrast Ratio

Deep blacks and bright whites deliver exceptional image clarity with high contrast for impactful visuals.

Tizen 7.0 OS

Built-in Tizen operating system enables powerful SoC performance with System-on-Chip functionality.

MagicINFO S10

Samsung's robust content management solution for easy remote management and content scheduling.

IP5x Dust Protection

Industrial-grade dust protection ensures reliable operation in various commercial environments.

Multi-Screen Support

Connect and control multiple displays seamlessly with RS232C and RJ45 LAN connectivity.

Wireless Display Mirroring

Built-in WiFi enables easy screen sharing from mobile devices without additional hardware.

Crystal UHD Display Technology

The QBC Series features Samsung's Crystal UHD technology delivering exceptional 4K resolution with Dynamic Crystal Color. The 350 nit brightness ensures clear visibility in typical commercial lighting conditions.

  • 3840 x 2160 UHD Resolution
  • Dynamic Crystal Color enhancement
  • 178° wide viewing angle

Smart Signage Platform

Powered by Tizen 7.0 with embedded MagicINFO S10, the QBC Series offers comprehensive content management capabilities. Deploy and manage content across multiple displays from a single interface.

  • Built-in media player
  • Remote content scheduling
  • Web browser for dynamic content

Reliable 16/7 Operation

Designed for 16 hours per day, 7 days a week operation with built-in temperature and humidity sensors. Auto Power On/Off functionality enables scheduled operation for energy efficiency.

  • Temperature sensor for protection
  • Humidity sensor (10~80%)
  • Auto source switching

Versatile Connectivity

Comprehensive input options including HDMI 2.0, USB 2.0, and network connectivity. Multiple mounting options with VESA compatibility for flexible installation across various environments.

  • HDMI 2.0 with HDCP 2.2
  • Built-in WiFi connectivity
  • RS232C & RJ45 for control
