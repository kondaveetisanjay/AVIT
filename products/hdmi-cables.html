Why Choose Our HDMI Cables & Connectivity

8K@60Hz Support

Ultra-high-definition 8K resolution at 60Hz refresh rate for crystal-clear visuals on next-gen displays.

CL3 In-Wall Rated

Fire-safety rated cables approved for permanent in-wall installation in commercial and residential spaces.

Gold-Plated Connectors

Corrosion-resistant gold-plated terminals ensure reliable signal transfer and long-lasting durability.

Active Optical Cables

Fiber-optic HDMI cables for ultra-long runs up to 100m without signal degradation or latency.

Shielded Design

Triple-layer shielding prevents electromagnetic interference for clean, artifact-free signal transmission.

Certified HDMI 2.1

Officially certified HDMI 2.1 cables supporting 48Gbps bandwidth, eARC, and dynamic HDR.

Various Lengths

Bulk Packaging

Product Categories

AV Cable Solutions

Standard HDMI Cables

High-speed HDMI cables for everyday AV connectivity. Supporting 4K@120Hz and 8K@60Hz with HDMI 2.1 certification for reliable, high-bandwidth signal delivery.

  • HDMI 2.1 / 48Gbps bandwidth
  • Gold-plated connectors
  • Available in 0.5m to 15m lengths

Active Optical HDMI

Fiber-optic HDMI cables for ultra-long-distance runs without signal loss. Lightweight, flexible, and immune to electromagnetic interference for mission-critical installations.

  • Runs up to 100 meters
  • Zero signal degradation
  • EMI-immune fiber core

HDMI over Ethernet / HDBaseT

HDBaseT extenders and converters that transmit 4K HDMI, audio, Ethernet, control signals, and power over a single Cat6 cable up to 100m.

  • 4K signal over Cat6 up to 100m
  • Integrated IR & RS-232 control
  • PoH (Power over HDBaseT)

Classroom AV Cables

Pre-labeled education bundles designed for smart classroom deployments. Complete cable kits with projector connection cables, color-coded labels, and standardized lengths for easy installation.

  • Pre-labeled education bundles
  • Cable kits for smart classrooms
  • Projector connection cables

USB-C to HDMI Adapters

Universal USB-C adapters for seamless laptop-to-display connectivity. Plug-and-play adapters supporting 4K@60Hz output from USB-C and Thunderbolt 3/4 devices.

  • 4K@60Hz output via USB-C
  • Thunderbolt 3/4 compatible
  • Plug-and-play, no drivers needed
