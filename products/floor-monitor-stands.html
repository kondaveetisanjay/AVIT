Key Features

Why Choose Our Floor Monitor Stands

Height Adjustable

Easily adjust screen height to achieve the perfect ergonomic viewing position for any user or environment.

Universal VESA

Compatible with all standard VESA mounting patterns from 75x75 to 800x400 for screens up to 100 inches.

Cable Management

Integrated cable routing channels keep power and signal cables neatly organized and hidden from view.

Heavy Duty Steel

Premium cold-rolled steel construction supports displays weighing up to 100 kg with rock-solid stability.

360° Rotation

Full swivel and tilt adjustment allows optimal screen positioning for any audience or viewing angle.

Anti-Tip Design

Weighted base and wide footprint ensure maximum stability even with large, heavy displays mounted.

Tool-Free Assembly

Quick and easy setup with no special tools required, reducing installation time to minutes.

Lockable Wheels

Heavy-duty casters with locking brakes for effortless mobility and secure stationary positioning.

Product Categories

Floor Stand Solutions

Mobile TV Stands

Portable rolling stands with lockable casters for easy repositioning of displays across rooms and spaces. Ideal for presentations, meetings, and flexible work environments.

  • Heavy-duty locking casters
  • Height adjustable columns
  • Built-in accessory shelves

Fixed Floor Stands

Stationary floor-mounted stands for permanent display installations. Ultra-stable weighted bases provide secure positioning for lobbies, showrooms, and dedicated viewing areas.

  • Weighted steel base for stability
  • Concealed cable management
  • Supports screens up to 86"

Dual / Multi-Screen Stands

Multi-display floor stands supporting side-by-side or stacked configurations for command centers, trading floors, and digital signage arrays.

  • Dual and triple screen support
  • Independent screen adjustment
  • Landscape and portrait modes

Education & Classroom Stands

Purpose-built stands for educational environments including height-adjustable models for teachers, interactive panel stands for collaborative learning, and mobile stands for classroom flexibility.

  • Height-adjustable for seated and standing use
  • Interactive flat panel compatible
  • Mobile stands for room-to-room sharing

Kiosk & Digital Signage Stands

Enclosed and open-frame kiosk stands for interactive wayfinding, digital menus, and advertising displays in high-traffic public spaces.

  • Tamper-resistant enclosures
  • Touchscreen compatible
  • Integrated media player shelf
