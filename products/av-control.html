Product Categories

Control Solutions

Touch Panel Controllers

Wall-mount and tabletop touch panels provide elegant control interfaces for meeting rooms and AV systems. Customizable interfaces adapt to any application with intuitive controls.

  • 7" to 15" touch displays
  • PoE powered for easy installation
  • Customizable GUI interfaces

Matrix Switchers

HDMI and HDBaseT matrix switchers route any source to any display in your facility. Seamless switching with 4K60 4:4:4 support and HDCP 2.2 compliance.

  • 4K60 HDR support
  • Scalers for mixed-resolution outputs
  • Audio breakaway and embedding

AV over IP

Network-based video distribution eliminates distance limitations and simplifies installation. Leverage existing network infrastructure for enterprise-wide AV distribution.

  • Unlimited scalability
  • Uses standard network switches
  • Video wall processing

Room Scheduling Panels

Digital door panels display room availability and enable instant booking. Sync with Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, and other calendar platforms.

  • LED status indicators
  • Calendar integration
  • Occupancy sensors
