Key Features

Why Choose Our Network Equipment

High-Power PoE

PoE++ (802.3bt) delivers up to 90W per port for powering PTZ cameras, displays, and AV devices.

High Bandwidth

10GbE uplinks and multi-gigabit ports support demanding AV over IP and video applications.

AV-Optimized

Pre-configured QoS and IGMP snooping profiles for reliable multicast video streaming.

Cloud Management

Centralized cloud dashboard for monitoring, configuration, and troubleshooting across all sites.

Enterprise Security

802.1X authentication, ACLs, and VLAN segmentation protect your network infrastructure.

Stackable Design

Stack multiple switches for simplified management and increased port density.

Redundancy

Link aggregation and STP for high availability in mission-critical AV applications.

Traffic Analytics

Deep visibility into network traffic for capacity planning and troubleshooting.

Product Categories

Network Solutions

PoE Network Switches

Power-over-Ethernet switches deliver data and power over a single cable, simplifying installation of IP cameras, VoIP phones, wireless APs, and AV devices.

  • PoE+ and PoE++ options
  • 8 to 48 port configurations
  • Fanless models for quiet spaces

AV Network Switches

Purpose-built switches for AV over IP with pre-configured multicast settings, low latency, and high-bandwidth support for demanding video applications.

  • IGMP snooping optimized
  • Non-blocking architecture
  • AV manufacturer certified

Wireless Access Points

Enterprise WiFi 6/6E access points for high-density environments. Support wireless presentation, mobile devices, and IoT with reliable, secure connectivity.

  • WiFi 6 and WiFi 6E support
  • MU-MIMO and OFDMA
  • Cloud or controller managed

Network Infrastructure

Routers, firewalls, and structured cabling form the backbone of reliable AV and IT networks. Enterprise-grade equipment for performance and security.

  • Enterprise routers and firewalls
  • Cat6A and fiber cabling
  • Rack and cable management
