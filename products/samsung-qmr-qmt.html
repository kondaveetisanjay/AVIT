Key Features

Why Choose Samsung QMR/QMT Series

Crystal Processor 4K

Samsung's powerful Crystal Processor 4K delivers stunning picture quality with intelligent upscaling and enhanced color accuracy.

500 nit Brightness

High brightness display ensures clear visibility even in brightly lit commercial environments with 4000:1 contrast ratio.

24/7 Continuous Operation

Built for demanding commercial applications with round-the-clock operation capability and reliable performance.

3-Bezel Free Design

Minimalistic bezel-free design on 3 sides creates seamless video walls and elegant standalone installations.

MagicINFO S10

Comprehensive content management solution for centralized control and scheduling across multiple displays.

USB-C Connectivity

Modern USB-C port with 65W power delivery enables single-cable connection for compatible devices (QMT Series).

Built-in WiFi 5

Integrated WiFi connectivity for wireless content delivery and network management without external adapters.

IP5x Protection

Dust-resistant design ensures reliable operation in various commercial and industrial environments.

Crystal Processor 4K Technology

Samsung's Crystal Processor 4K uses powerful AI-based algorithms to transform everything you watch into stunning 4K. Advanced upscaling technology enhances lower resolution content to near-4K quality.

  • AI-powered picture enhancement
  • Intelligent color mapping
  • Content-adaptive brightness control

Premium 3-Bezel Free Design

The 3-bezel free design minimizes visual distractions by eliminating bezels on three sides. Perfect for creating immersive video walls or elegant single-display installations.

  • Near-seamless video wall creation
  • Modern, sleek aesthetics
  • Easy edge-to-edge content alignment

Enterprise-Grade 24/7 Operation

Engineered for continuous 24/7 operation, the QMR/QMT Series delivers reliable performance in the most demanding commercial environments. Built-in sensors monitor temperature and humidity for optimal operation.

  • Thermal management system
  • Auto source switching on failure
  • Industrial-grade components

Comprehensive Connectivity

Multiple input options including HDMI 2.0, DisplayPort, USB-C (QMT), and network connectivity provide flexibility for any installation scenario. RS232 and RJ45 enable centralized control.

  • HDMI 2.0 with HDCP 2.2 support
  • DisplayPort 1.2 input
  • USB-C 65W power delivery (QMT)
