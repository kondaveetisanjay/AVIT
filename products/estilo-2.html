Key Features

Why Choose Estilo 2.0

Estilo Series is a large screen offered as an all-in-one package including an embedded controller with built-in Android operating system and built-in speakers. Simply turn on with a remote control like a TV.

Inbuilt Android Media Player

Android 9.0 operating system for seamless app integration and content playback.

Wireless Mirroring

Effortlessly mirror screens from any compatible device without cables or adapters.

Full HD Resolution

1920 x 1080 resolution with crisp, detailed visuals for immersive viewing experience.

Cinematic Viewing Angle

170° horizontal and vertical viewing angles ensure visibility from any position.

Built-in Speakers

Powerful 30W x 2 (60W total) speakers eliminate the need for external audio.

Remote Control

Simple operation with included remote control - use it just like a TV.

Plug and Play

No complex configuration needed. Quick setup and instant operation.

Front-Accessible

Easy front access for maintenance without removing the display from the wall.

Ultra Thin - Sleek Design

The Estilo series is exceptionally sleek, seamlessly integrating with the wall for a modern, minimalist aesthetic. The ultra-slim profile makes it perfect for any contemporary space.

  • Ultra-slim profile design
  • Seamless wall integration
  • Modern aesthetic appeal

Wall Mount / Floor Mount

The Estilo series offers the flexibility of both wall mount and floor mount installation, providing customers with a variety of options to suit their space and preference.

  • Wall mount installation option
  • Floor mount with mobile stand
  • Versatile placement options

Viewing Comfort

The Estilo series boasts Full HD resolution, providing crisp and detailed visuals for an immersive viewing experience. It prioritizes viewing comfort, ensuring users can enjoy extended screen time without eye strain.

  • Full HD 1920 x 1080 resolution
  • Anti-fatigue viewing technology
  • Extended viewing comfort

Seamless Switch & Content Preview

The Estilo series offers seamless input switching and content preview capabilities, allowing users to effortlessly switch between different input sources without any interruptions or delays.

  • 3 HDMI input ports
  • USB-C connectivity
  • Instant source switching

Wireless Screen Sharing

The Estilo series incorporates advanced wireless screen sharing features, allowing users to effortlessly mirror their screens from compatible devices without cables or additional accessories.

  • Multi-device screen mirroring
  • No additional dongles required
  • Supports laptops, tablets, phones
