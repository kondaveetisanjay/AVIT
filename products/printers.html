Key Features

Why Choose Our Printers & Copiers

High-Speed Output

Print speeds up to 75 ppm for laser and 100 ppm for production inkjet to meet demanding workloads.

Professional Quality

Sharp text and vibrant colors with up to 1200 x 1200 dpi resolution for professional documents.

Network Integration

Ethernet and WiFi connectivity with support for mobile printing from any device.

Secure Printing

PIN release, card authentication, and encrypted data transmission protect sensitive documents.

Eco-Efficient

Energy Star certified with automatic duplex printing to reduce paper and power consumption.

High Capacity

Large paper trays and high-yield toner cartridges minimize maintenance and downtime.

Cloud Printing

Print from Google Cloud Print, Apple AirPrint, and Microsoft Universal Print.

Fleet Management

Centralized monitoring and management tools for IT administrators.

Product Categories

Printer Solutions

Desktop Laser Printers

Compact laser printers for personal desks and small workgroups. Fast output, low cost per page, and reliable performance for everyday business printing.

  • Up to 40 ppm print speed
  • Compact footprint
  • USB, Ethernet, WiFi connectivity

Workgroup Printers

High-capacity printers for shared departmental use. Higher speeds, larger paper capacity, and enhanced security features for busy offices.

  • 40-60 ppm print speed
  • Multiple paper trays
  • Secure print release

Production Printers

High-volume production systems for print rooms and central reprographics departments. Maximum throughput with professional finishing options.

  • 75+ ppm print speed
  • Inline finishing options
  • Variable data printing

Wide Format Printers

Large format inkjet printers for posters, banners, CAD drawings, and signage. Vibrant color output on various media types up to 64" wide.

  • Up to 64" print width
  • Photo-quality output
  • Roll and sheet media
