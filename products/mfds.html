Product Categories

MFD Solutions

Desktop MFDs

Compact multifunction devices for small offices and workgroups. Essential print, scan, copy, and fax capabilities in a space-saving design.

  • Up to 35 ppm print speed
  • 50-sheet document feeder
  • WiFi and mobile printing

Floor-Standing MFDs

Departmental multifunction systems for busy offices requiring high-volume output, advanced scanning, and professional finishing options.

  • 50-75 ppm print speed
  • 100+ sheet RADF
  • Inline stapling & hole punch

Color Production MFDs

High-volume color systems for central reprographics, marketing departments, and in-house print shops requiring professional color quality.

  • 75+ ppm color printing
  • Professional color management
  • Advanced finishing modules

A3 Workgroup MFDs

Large-format multifunction devices that handle A3 (11x17") documents alongside standard sizes, ideal for engineering, architecture, and design.

  • A3 and A4 support
  • CAD and GIS printing
  • Large document scanning
