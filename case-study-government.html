State Command Center

Complete AV transformation of 24/7 conference rooms with integrated video conferencing and collaboration tools across their new headquarters.

24/7 Operations
99.9% Uptime
3 Month Project
100% Client Satisfaction

The Challenge

Tech Corp, a leading technology company, was moving into a new 200,000 sq ft headquarters. They needed to equip 24/7 conference rooms with state-of-the-art AV technology that would support their hybrid workforce and enhance collaboration across global teams.

Key challenges included:

  • Standardizing AV systems across diverse room sizes
  • Ensuring seamless integration with Microsoft Teams
  • Meeting tight construction timeline
  • Staying within budget while delivering premium quality

Our Solution

AVIT designed and implemented a comprehensive AV solution that addressed all of Tech Corp's requirements:

Unified Communication System

We deployed Microsoft Teams Rooms certified systems in all conference spaces, ensuring consistent user experience across the organization. Each room features:

  • Interactive displays with touch capabilities
  • Professional-grade cameras with AI tracking
  • Ceiling microphone arrays for crystal-clear audio
  • One-touch meeting join functionality

Executive Boardroom

The flagship boardroom received a custom-designed LED videowall spanning 16 feet, integrated with advanced control systems and premium audio for an impressive meeting experience.

Collaboration Spaces

Open collaboration areas were equipped with wireless presentation systems and interactive displays, enabling spontaneous meetings and creative brainstorming sessions.

Results

The implementation delivered exceptional results:

  • 99.9% increase in meeting efficiency
  • 60% reduction in IT support tickets for AV issues
  • 95% adoption rate within first month
  • Zero downtime since deployment
"AVIT transformed our meeting spaces beyond our expectations. The seamless technology has become invisible - it just works. Our teams can now focus on collaboration instead of troubleshooting." - CTO, Tech Corp
